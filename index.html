Piano Teaching Method

I believe in a 100% positive reinforcement teaching approach. My lessons are designed to help you progress quickly while keeping the learning experience engaging. We cover your favorite music, dive into music theory, and even explore creative composition in a professional setting—whether in my studio or at your home.

Gavin Hammond Biography

With over 30 years of piano experience and a deep understanding of various musical styles, Gavin Hammond offers transformative lessons. A graduate of the Los Angeles Film School in music production, Gavin has excelled as a session musician for 18 years, composing music for singers and media alike. His dedication and passion for teaching make every lesson a unique musical journey.
